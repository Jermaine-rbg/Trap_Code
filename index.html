TRAP CODE

This podcast will be used to share truth, creativity and encouragement with listeners to nurture, heal and inspire them — and in doing so, help make the world a better and more empathetic place.

The "Trap Code" Inspirational Podcast will cover topics that those from the inner city and underrepresented communities can relate to and voice their testimony on breaking into tech.

  • What does it takes to land a role in tech?
  • Who is underrepresented in tech?
  • Why does diversity in tech matter?

Voices from the underrepresented communities need to be heard, the challenges that most people don’t face, the determination to succeed in a environment that most call the Trap.
